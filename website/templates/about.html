About us

  • home
  • About

FLIPOFFICE is India's first technology and process Enabled Office Expansion Management Venture.

The current landscape of Office Expansion is fragmented and unstructured with startup and SME companies having to deal separately with multiple stakeholders including real estate brokers, builders, interior designer, PMCs, and Contractors. FLIPOFFICE focuses on easing this pain-point by becoming a one-stop solution to a brand's office expansion needs. This is done through a 3 step process for Brands which includes;

DISCOVER - Helping brands freeze on an office space mapped to their requirements of location, function, and budget through a data and tech-driven approach

DESIGN - Using VR-Tech enabled design process to collaboratively create an amazing theme based office for the client within budget and timelines

EXECUTE - End to end contracting and execution services driven by water-tight project management
